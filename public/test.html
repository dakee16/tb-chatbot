Tilesbay Chatbot — Local Test

Click the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner to open the widget. Try these test prompts to verify each feature.

Test 1 — Coverage calculator (no Magento needed)

Type this into the chat:

I'm tiling a bathroom that's 8 ft by 10 ft. How much tile do I need?

Expected: bot calls calculate_tile_coverage, returns 80 sqft + 10% waste = 88 sqft, suggests boxes.

Test 2 — Product recommender (Magento required)

Type this into the chat:

Show me some matte white subway tiles for a kitchen backsplash.

Expected: bot calls search_products, returns 3–5 matching tiles with name, price, URL.

Test 3 — Combined flow

Type this into the chat:

I have a 12x14 living room floor I want to tile in a rustic wood-look. What do you recommend and how many boxes do I need?

Expected: bot searches catalog, recommends tiles, asks for sqft-per-box or calculates with default 10.
